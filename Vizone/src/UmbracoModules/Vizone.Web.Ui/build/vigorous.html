Healthcheck

We have now been involved in many delivery programmes good and bad, this is our bread and butter and it is what we are good at. We have over time developed a toolkit that ensures we continue to successfully deliver programmes. Healthcheck is Vizone’s toolkit which analyses the strengths and weaknesses of a business transformation programme against key benchmarking scores from similar programmes across the world. Our Healthcheck allows the significance for each of these scores to be tailored to the client’s business needs and identifies in priority order the key areas to be improved and the effort required to deliver the programme and, ultimately, increase shareholder value. Healthcheck will identify key areas for improvement, develop an action plan for recovery or implementation and accurately define the resources needed to deliver on time and on budget. Assurance

Assurance is Vizone’s integrity toolset which ensures that all aspects of a business transformation programme conform to the initial plan, has engaged the correct people and is in line with required standards, regulations and budgets. Assurance covers all aspects of the change programme including business, user and supplier and aims to widen the key success factors for a project beyond delivery, which focuses on timescales, to ensure that other critical success factors are accomplished. Key to this is the Programme Assurance Manager whose role is to police the project to ensure the project is driven in line with all success factors. Recovery

Recovery is Vizone’s business transformation turnaround service, which aims to literally, turn around failing business change programmes. Recovery goes to the heart of the issues which have derailed a business transformation. It identifies and prioritises the key actions which will put it back on track quickly and the critical governance and controls needed to deliver the programme. Business change can fail for a multitude of reasons, from team dynamics through to resourcing, supplier failure, technological issues, financial pressures, communication breakdowns and even lack of support for the programme in key departments of an organisation. Vizone has experience of successfully overcoming all of these issues and putting robust plans and actions in place to recover the momentum in a business transformation programme. Mobilisation

The Mobilisation phase takes an embryonic business Concept or Vision through a controlled, rapid and typically time-boxed process to emerge with a fully formed and viable programme, ready to deliver the objectives and benefits that underpin the initial Concept/Vision. Within the Vizone Way, a Vizone International led Mobilisation phase is instigated following a Healthcheck. The rationale for instigating a Mobilisation rather than a Recovery will be driven by the maturity of the programme’s formation, teaming and executive sponsorship. Where the programme is without shape, momentum or direction, Mobilisation may be the better way of galvanising stakeholders and teams in readiness for Delivery.

Delivery of business change requires a clear methodology which includes the scoping, managing and monitoring of programmes across our range of services from strategic planning, through to acquisitions and joint ventures, business planning, asset management, risk management, procurement and resourcing, business process engineering, strategic systems and data architecture and knowledge management. Handback

The Hand Back phase closes down the Vizone International engagement with a programme and follows Recovery, Mobilisation or Delivery phase involvement. During this phase control of the programme is handed back to Customer management teams for onward delivery and any collateral generated by the Vizone International team is handed over to Customer representatives. Within the Vizone Way, the Hand Back phase is the final phase of Vizone International’s engagement with a programme and is effected when the objectives of the engagement are met. Typically, the engagement will end once a pre-agreed point in the programme is reached, a period of stability is established and Customer teams are operating effectively.
